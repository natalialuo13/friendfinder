About us

Friend Finder is a web-based application that helps users to find friends in the environment of Sam Ratulangi University campus. With easy-to-use features such as searching for friends by simply entering keywords of name, skills, or hobbies, users can find friends who have the same skills or hobbies. Users can also update their daily activities, which can be seen by other users. Friend Finder is here to make it easier for you who have difficulties in finding friends without draining your time and energy !
